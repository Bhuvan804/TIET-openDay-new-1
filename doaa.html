Profile:

Dr. S. S. Bhatia, Professor in Mathematics has been serving as Dean of Academic Affairs (DOAA) since September 2014. His academic journey at TIET started in the Year 1987 when he joined as Lecturer in School of Mathematics . He has been Head, School of Mathematics twice before joining as DOAA. He has been teaching various courses in engineering mathematics at TIET. His areas of specialisation are Fourier Series & Functional Analysis. His research interests include Fixed point theory, L-1 Convergence of trigonometric series and Sequence spaces. He has guided 14 PhD students and has more than 100 publications in journals of international and national repute. He has undertaken various Govt. aided Research projects at TIET.
