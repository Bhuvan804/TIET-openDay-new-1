Dr. Inderveer Channa

Dean of Student Affairs

Date & Time: 21 Sept 2020, 10:00 AM


At Thapar, we believe that holistic learning is unequivocally the foundation of growth. In academics, in addition to learning engineering fundamentals we endorse critical thinking, problem-solving and communication skills, essential to lifelong learning. We tap and channelize the intellectual potential to create high quality engineers. We uphold a student centric approach, inculcating values of integrity, cohesiveness and synergy. The extra-curricular activities organized at Thapar serve as a breeding ground for future leaders. Each activity is an experience tailored for students to pursue their interests and showcase their talent and skill. While getting involved in extra-curricular activities and exploring new interests, students build new bonds of friendship and establish their on-campus clusters. These interactions not only indoctrinate managerial prowess but also help students in honing their interpersonal skills. A home away from home, this vibrant campus offers many opulent hostels with contemporary facilities. A lush green campus with a community of inspired and talented people coupled with an effervescent ambience offers a perfect student life. Live it to believe it.
