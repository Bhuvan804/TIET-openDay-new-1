Dr. Prem Pal Bansal

Head of Department, Department of Civil Engineering

Welcome to the Department of Civil Engineering. I am delighted that you are showcasing your interest to know more about us, virtue being this web presence. This web-site holds information for all the stakeholders. We at Civil department offer one of the best Civil Engineering undergraduate program aimed at the super-creative generation.

In BE Civil Engineering, Department offers three focus areas namely: Structural Engineering and Sustainable Materials, Smart cities, Infrastructure Development and Management. Our undergraduate curriculum is a perfect balance between theoretical requirements and practical aspects of not just the broad disciplines of civil engineering such as structure, surveying, geotechnical, transportation, and water resources engineering but also provides the management and entrepreneurial scope in its coverage.
